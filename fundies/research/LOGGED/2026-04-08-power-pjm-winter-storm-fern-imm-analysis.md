# PJM IMM — Winter Storm Fern Market Operations Analysis (MIC, 2026-04-08)

## Summary

The IMM presented a 36-page analysis of market operations during Winter Storm Fern at the April 8 MIC meeting. This is the detailed post-mortem of the January 2026 winter event that drove record PJM-W congestion, the $23/MWh geopolitical risk premium in NQ26 sparks, and record FG26 AD-West spreads of -$38/MWh.
