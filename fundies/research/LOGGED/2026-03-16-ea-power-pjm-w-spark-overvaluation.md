# PJM-W Summer Sparks — Geopolitical Risk Premium vs Fair Value

## Summary

EA flags PJM-W NQ26 summer sparks as overvalued, settling $4.64/MWh higher w/w on geopolitical risk premium rather than fundamentals. Current levels above $69/MWh are well above EA's fair value estimate of ~$46/MWh. The rally is positioning-driven: NQ26 delivered M3 forwards rose in tandem with fixed gas (the ~$5.50/MWh West Hub rally tracks gas, not load), and CFTC data shows significantly higher y/y open interest on PJM-W ICE contracts. Summer has traded $9+/MWh higher since Winter Storm Fern and would be a record realisation. TTF rising 20% w/w is a lingering coal risk via increased US Atlantic basin exports.

## Key Points

- **PJM-W NQ26 sparks**: Settled $4.64/MWh higher w/w; trading above **$69/MWh**
- **EA fair value**: ~**$46/MWh** — market pricing ~$23/MWh of risk premium
- **Positioning-driven**: M3 forwards rose in tandem with fixed gas; ~$5.50/MWh West Hub rally tracks gas, not load fundamentals
- **CFTC open interest**: PJM-W ICE contract OI significantly higher y/y — more speculation or hedging activity
- **Winter Storm Fern effect**: Summer has traded **$9+/MWh higher** since Fern; would be a record realisation at current levels
- **Coal risk**: TTF +20% w/w incentivises higher US thermal coal exports into the Atlantic basin, lifting PJM summer coal-on-margin pricing

## Trading Implications

- **Bearish signal at current levels** — EA sees ~$23/MWh of overvaluation ($69 vs $46 fair value); positioning-driven rallies tend to unwind
- **Watch M3 vs fixed gas divergence** — if M3 decouples to the upside from fixed gas, that would signal fundamental tightening vs pure positioning; tandem movement = speculative
- **Coal is the tail risk that could justify some premium** — TTF rally → US export call → higher delivered coal → higher PJM summer if coal stays on the margin; this was flagged in the Mar 15 outlook as the "sleeper risk"
- **CFTC OI as contrarian indicator** — elevated speculative positioning at record-level sparks is a setup for a sharp unwind if geopolitical risk fades
- **Record realisation framing** — $9/MWh above pre-Fern levels; market is pricing in a scenario that has never historically materialised
